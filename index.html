ABOUT OUR SCHOOL

CPEC SAINT BABETH TSS is a modern secondary school located in Byumba, Rwanda. The school provides quality education for both Ordinary Level and Advanced Level students.

Our mission is to prepare students for future careers through discipline, innovation and professional technical skills.

We focus on developing responsible students who are ready for the modern digital world.
